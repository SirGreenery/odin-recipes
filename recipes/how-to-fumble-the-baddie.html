So What is This?

A time-tested recipe for disaster to turn any potential connection into a memerable mess for every party involved. Even if you follow the recipe loosly, you can still fumble the baddie like the game winning touchdown at the Super Bowl.

Instructions

  1. Before even going to school/work, do not shower for at least 3 days. Make sure your Elon Musk can be from across a room. Ignore brushing teeth and tounge to add a breath that punches someone in the nose.
  2. Once you arrive, spot the baddie across the room and make your approach. Do not do any last minutes hygeine checks nor take gum/peppermint to mask your breath. You may start sweating during the interaction, this is what we want. Armpit stains build character.
  3. Introduce yourself and compliment their looks. Something weird like say their feet are pretty even when their wearing closed toed shoes. Don't say they dressed nice because that would make too much sense. Instead, compliment them on something they had no control over. A generic "You're beautiful." is a classic method to drop the ball as fast as possible.
  4. If they seem reserved, that means your body odor is working. Keep going. At this point, they may think you either want money from them (because you smell like you're homeless) or you're a serial killer. Capitalize on this and add nervous laughter at non-jokes if they try to relieve the tension.
    • If they say they already have a partner, tell them they look like they could use two.
  5. Watch them try to nicely leave the conversation. Bonus points if you follow them to the point of catching a charge.

Chef's Note: For best results, keep using this recipe until you either give up dating all together or accidentally stumble into a person that matches your freak. Cheers bruv.
